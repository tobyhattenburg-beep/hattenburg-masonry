Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Straight answers on licensing, service area, estimates, and how we work.

Are you a licensed masonry contractor?

Yes. Hattenburg Masonry LLC holds an active California C-29 Masonry Contractor license (#1066248), which covers the full range of cementitious applications — from structural block to finish stonework.

What areas do you serve?

We serve Greater Los Angeles, including Pasadena, Glendale, Burbank, La Cañada Flintridge, San Marino, Arcadia, Sherman Oaks, Studio City, Los Feliz, and Silver Lake. See the service-area list above.

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes. Request a consultation through the form on this site or call 323-542-1884, and we'll review your project and follow up — typically within one business day — to schedule a walkthrough and estimate.

How long does a typical masonry project take?

It depends on scope. A stone-veneer fireplace or feature wall may take one to two weeks, while a full façade or structural project runs longer. We give a realistic schedule with your estimate and keep you updated throughout.

Can you match the color of existing mortar?

Yes. We use exact Solomon & Davis mortar color matching so repairs, additions, and repointing blend seamlessly into the original work.

Do you take on both residential and commercial work?

Our focus is high-end residential masonry for homeowners, architects, and builders, though we also take on select commercial and architectural projects. Reach out with your details and we'll let you know if it's a fit.
